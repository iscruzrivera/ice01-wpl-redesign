Worcester Performative Library’s Most Performative Books of 2024

Here it is: the definitive syllabus for guys in jorts who drink iced matcha like it’s a personality trait. These are the top 10 most performative books for young men in their early 20s — the same ones you’ll see sticking out of a tote bag underlined just enough to imply you “get it.” Bonus points if you casually let your wired earphones dangle while quoting feminist literature between Clairo and Beabadoobee tracks. 

Our Adult Reading Recommendations? Quarterly drops that feel like a Labubu blind box pull — unpredictable, collectable, and ideal for a passive-aggressive Instagram story caption. And if you’re more into algorithms than vibes, check out Novelist Plus: a database where you can search for books that are “like Sally Rooney but with more ennui” or “something that makes me look complex on public transit.” Or, you know, just swing by the reference desk and ask us. We’ll happily supply you with the perfect book to strategically leave half-read on your nightstand.
